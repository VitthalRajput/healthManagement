What We Monitor

5 key health indicators tracked simultaneously using precision IoT sensors

❤️

Heart Rate

Continuous pulse monitoring using photoplethysmography (PPG) sensors for accurate BPM readings.

Normal: 60–100 BPM
🩸

SpO₂ — Oxygen Level

Blood oxygen saturation tracking using pulse oximetry to detect hypoxia early.

Normal: 95–100%
🌡️

Body Temperature

Infrared and contact sensors to monitor temperature fluctuations and detect fever.

Normal: 36.1–37.2°C
🍬

Blood Sugar

Non-invasive glucose level estimation for diabetic patient monitoring and alerts.

Normal: 70–99 mg/dL
🌬️

Respiratory Rate

Breathing pattern analysis to detect respiratory distress or irregularities in real time.

Normal: 12–20 /min
🤖

AI Analysis

Logical AI processing of all vitals together to generate health status and automated summaries.

Normal / Warning / Critical
How It Works

From Sensor to Insight

Three seamless steps from data collection to medical decision support

Sensors Capture

Precision medical sensors collect heart rate, SpO₂, temperature, blood sugar & respiratory rate

🔌

ESP32 Transmits
